The brown-mandibled aracari (US /ˌɑːrəˈsɑːri/ AHR-ə-SAHR-ee,[1]UK /ˌɑːrəˈsɑːri/ ARR-ə-SAHR-ee or /ˌɑːrəˈkɑːri/ ARR-ə-KAHR-ee),[2] or brown-mandibled araçari (Pteroglossus mariae), is a species of bird in the Ramphastidae family. It is found in Bolivia, Brazil, and Peru. The brown-mandibled aracari is still considered to be a subspecies of the ivory-billed aracari by some authourities.[3]
